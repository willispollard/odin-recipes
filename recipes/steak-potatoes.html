Instructions for making steak & mashed potatoes:

Ingredients:

  • Steak
  • Gold potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps:

  1. Pat steaks dry with paper towl
  2. Season steaks with salt and pepper, and rosemary and/or thyme for added flavor
  3. Heat cast iron pan over medium-high heat
  4. Boil water in a medium-large pot
  5. Chop potatoes with skin still on
  6. When water comes to a boil, cook potatoes until soft
  7. Butter pan while heating
  8. When hot, begin cooking steaks for 2-4 minutes on each side (including edges, use tongs)
  9. Remove from heat when cooked to liking
  10. Strain finished potatoes
  11. Mash potatoes with butter, sour cream, milk, cheese, and salt/pepper
  12. Serve steak and potatoes on place, topping steak with butter
  13. Enjoy!
