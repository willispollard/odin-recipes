Recipe for my special Bolognese:

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Rigatoni
  • Rao's Tomato Sauce (Rec: Roasted Garlic)
  • Onion
  • Bell Peppers
  • Sliced white or baby bella mushrooms
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Chop onion, bell peppers, and mushrooms (if not sliced)
  2. Cook ground beef on stove over medium-high heat
  3. Concurrently, boil water in a medium-sized pot
  4. Once water comes to a boil, cook at lease one entire box of rigatoni (al dente preferred)
  5. Season ground beef with chili powder, garlic powder, chili flakes, and italian seasoning
  6. Add contents of Rao's tomato sauce to ground beef and mix together
  7. Reduce heat and bring to a simmer while mixing
  8. Strain pasta once cooked and mix with butter/oil
  9. Serve pasta in dish of choice
  10. Serve finished meat sauce over the top
  11. Finish with sprinkled parmesan cheese
  12. Enjoy!
