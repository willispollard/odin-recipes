Recipe for my signature Taco Bowl:

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • Tortilla chips
  • Black beans
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Jalapeño
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa of choice
  • Lime juice
  • Sour cream
  • Optional: Valentica hot sauce

Steps:

  1. Chop onion, tomato, and Jalapeño
  2. Cook ground beef on stove over medium-high heat
  3. Season ground beef with chili powder, garlic powder, and cumin
  4. In a dish or bowl, place a serving of each chopped vegetable
  5. Add black beans, salsa, sour cream, lime juice, and hot sauce (if desired)
  6. Once beef is fully cooked, scoop a serving onto dish
  7. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the hot beef so it melts
  8. Add lettuce and tortilla chips
  9. Mix ingredients and enjoy!
